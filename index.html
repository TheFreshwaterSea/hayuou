The following examples are some suggestions that can help you correct your hunchback:

Muscle exercise: Strengthening the back and core muscles can help improve posture and strengthen back support. Some common exercises include Push-up, bridge sports and plank support.

Maintain correct posture: Be aware of your posture issues and strive to maintain the correct posture, such as keeping your chest and head straight, and keeping your back straight.

Avoid prolonged sitting posture: Long sitting posture can easily lead to hunchback. It is recommended to get up and exercise every hour, and do some stretching exercises.

Using corrective equipment: There are some special corrective equipment that can help you maintain the correct posture, such as a hunchback corrective belt or a corrective vest that supports the back.
